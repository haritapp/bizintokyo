## Overview

Tokyo Big Sight (Tokyo International Exhibition Center) is one of Japan's largest exhibition venues, located in the Ariake area. With its iconic inverted-pyramid conference tower and massive exhibition halls, it hosts the country's most significant business events, trade shows, and conferences year-round.

## Access

Direct connection from Tokyo Big Sight Station on the Yurikamome line, and a 7-minute walk from Kokusai-Tenjijo Station on the Rinkai line. Convenient bus services from Haneda and Narita airports make it accessible for international visitors.

## Major Events

Tokyo Big Sight serves as the main venue for the Tokyo Motor Show, Comic Market, the Tokyo International Gift Show, and many global trade shows, welcoming millions of attendees every year.
