## First Wave of Speakers

The first lineup of keynote speakers for this spring's flagship business events has been announced. The roster includes CXOs from major global tech firms, executives from leading Japanese companies, and key policy makers.

## Track Structure

The program is organized into four tracks: the main stage, industry-specific tracks, a startup pitch track, and English-language sessions for international attendees. Detailed programs for each track will be released on a rolling basis.

## Tickets

Early-bird tickets are now on sale on the official website. Dedicated intake counters for press passes and exhibitor passes are also open, so please register according to your attendance type.
