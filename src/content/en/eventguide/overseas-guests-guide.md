## Entry and Airport Access

From Narita and Haneda airports, you can reach central Tokyo by Narita Express, Keisei Skyliner, Keikyu Line, limousine bus, or taxi. Travel time and cost vary considerably, so choose based on your hotel location and luggage volume.

## Getting Around with Suica / PASMO

Tokyo has an extensive network of JR, subway, and private rail lines. A single IC card (Suica, PASMO, or Welcome Suica) covers nearly all public transit. Taxi apps such as GO and Uber are also widely available near venues.

## Accommodation and Business Etiquette

International-brand hotels cluster near the major venues, Shinagawa, Shinjuku, and Marunouchi. Basic business etiquette — exchanging business cards properly, arriving on time, and a slight bow — will significantly improve your impression locally.
