## Urban Infrastructure and Venue Density

Tokyo is one of the rare global cities where large-scale venues, mid-size halls, and premium hotels are clustered within just a few kilometers. Tokyo Big Sight, Makuhari Messe, Tokyo International Forum, and the luxury hotels in Shinagawa and Marunouchi give organizers a broad range of choices by scale and purpose.

## Transit Access and Punctuality

Tokyo's rail network density and on-time performance are among the best in the world. Smooth airport access means minimal travel stress for international VIPs — a key strength for the city.

## Hospitality and Safety

Attendee experience is elevated by hospitable staff, a high level of public safety, and 24-hour food and retail infrastructure. These intangibles are, arguably, the real backbone of Tokyo's competitiveness as an event destination.
