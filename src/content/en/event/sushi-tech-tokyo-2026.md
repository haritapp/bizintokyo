## Event Overview

SusHi Tech Tokyo 2026 is one of the world's largest urban startup and technology events, hosted by the Tokyo Metropolitan Government. Startups, investors, government leaders, and researchers from around the globe gather in Tokyo to co-create the future city under the themes of sustainability and high technology.

## Dates and Venues

The main venue is Tokyo Big Sight, with satellite venues across the city. Pitch contests, exhibitions, and conferences run in parallel over multiple days.

## Featured Sessions

City leaders, top startups, and mobility / cleantech executives from many countries are confirmed to speak. English-language sessions and matchmaking programs are well prepared for international attendees. See the official site for the full program.
